Los Angeles is a world-class art city.

## 1. The Getty Center
*   **Why go:** Stunning architecture by Richard Meier, incredible views of LA, and European art.
*   **Cost:** Free admission (Parking $20).
*   **Tip:** Take the tram up the hill for the experience.

## 2. LACMA (Los Angeles County Museum of Art)
*   **Why go:** The "Urban Light" installation (the 202 lamp posts) outside is the most Instagrammed spot in LA.
*   **Cost:** $25 for non-residents. Free after 3 PM on weekdays for residents.

*   **Why go:** For movie lovers. See actual Oscars, costumes from Star Wars, and Dorothy's ruby slippers.
*   **Cost:** $25.
